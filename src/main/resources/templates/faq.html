Frequently Asked Questions

How can we help you?

Account & Billing

To create an account, simply click on the "Sign Up" button at the top of the page, fill in your details, and follow the instructions. You will need a valid email address and a strong password. Once your account is created, you can manage bookings, payments, and view your rental history.

You can update your payment information by logging into your account and navigating to the "Billing" section. Here, you can add, remove, or modify your payment methods. All payment information is securely encrypted for your protection.

Your rental history is accessible from your account dashboard. Navigate to the "Rentals" tab, where you can see a detailed list of all your past and current rentals, along with invoices and rental dates.

Website Issues

If you've forgotten your password, click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. Enter your email address, and we'll send you a link to reset your password. Make sure to choose a strong, unique password for your account security.

If you're unable to access certain pages, it may be due to your account status or browser settings. Ensure that you are logged in and that your browser allows cookies and JavaScript. If the problem persists, contact our support team for assistance.

If you encounter a technical issue, please go to our "Support" page and submit a ticket detailing the problem. Provide as much information as possible, including screenshots if necessary. Our technical team will work to resolve the issue promptly.
